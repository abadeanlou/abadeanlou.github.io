About

I'm a data scientist and engineer based in Milan, working where machine learning, geospatial data, and urban mobility meet. At CitiEU I design and build data platforms that turn city-scale movement data into models, maps, and decisions — from real-time traffic ingestion and ML travel-time inference to the transport models behind them.

Before that, I did research at Institut Polytechnique de Paris on public transit equity, published as first author at hEART 2022 and TRB 2023.
